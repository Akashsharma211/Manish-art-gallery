Manish Agrahari

Dr. by profession artist by passion.

Manish Agrahari is a self-taught artist whose journey began in the quiet corners of his childhood. With no formal training, he embraced art as a means of expression, allowing his imagination to run wild. His work is characterized by vibrant colors and whimsical themes that evoke a sense of nostalgia and wonder. Manish's art invites viewers to reconnect with their inner child, celebrating creativity and the joy of discovery. Through his unique perspective, he transforms everyday moments into extraordinary visual stories, making art accessible and relatable to all.
